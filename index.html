Welcome to software engineering at the brand new campus in the center of Horsens. To help you start your academic journey, you can find out about the courses you will attend during the first semester. We have provided an overview of the material you will cover as well as the skills you will gain as a result. We have also included a helpful timetable that shows an overview of what your semester will look like.

As a software engineer, you will apply the principles of engineering to software. As we depend more and more on electronic devices, it is critical that we understand how the logic behind them functions. During the course of your studies you will learn how to implement, manipulate, and test software. As you grow into this exciting field, you will encounter many challenging projects and interesting obstacles to overcome.
